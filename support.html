Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my package?
You can track your package by visiting our Track page and entering your tracking number. You can find your tracking number in the shipping confirmation email or SMS.
What are your delivery timelines?
Our delivery timelines depend on the service type and destination. Express Delivery is typically 1-2 business days, while Standard Shipping can take 3-5 business days. Same Day Delivery is available in select cities.
How do I ship a package with KXPRESS?
You can ship a package through our website by visiting the Ship page (coming soon), or by visiting one of our physical branches. You'll need to provide the recipient's details and package information.
What is the maximum weight and size for packages?
Our standard service accepts packages up to 30kg in weight. The maximum dimensions are 100cm x 80cm x 80cm. For larger or heavier items, please contact our customer support for specialized shipping arrangements.
My package is delayed. What should I do?
If your package is delayed, first check the tracking status for any updates. If there's no explanation for the delay, please contact our customer support team with your tracking number, and we'll investigate the issue for you.
